Why Use a Client-Side Image Compressor?

Using an in-browser image compressor offers the best speed and security.

Benefits of Client-Side Compression:

  • **Maximum Privacy:** Your images are never uploaded to a server; all processing occurs locally.
  • **Zero Latency:** Compression is instant, limited only by your device's CPU.
  • **SEO Advantage:** Smaller image files help your website load faster, improving your Google ranking.
  • **Cost-Effective:** Saves your bandwidth and server costs.
